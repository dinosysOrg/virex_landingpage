Golden City An Giang Urban Area
My Hoa Ward, Long Xuyen City, An Giang Province
PROJECT TYPE
A complex of offices, sports, services, trade fairs and residential areas
INVESTOR
Trading and Service Joint Stock Company (of Alphanam Group)
PRICE
Land price: 15.5 million VND/m2
Housing completion: 3.5-storey building
INTRODUCTION
The Golden City project in An Giang is currently in the process of selling land plots in stages. These plots are to be developed by Alphanam Group, or the land plot owner can assign their own developer should they wish to do so. The project is divided up into several sectors, each starting construction once all plots have been sold in their respective sector. This is done to ensure that residents will live in a vibrant community with every facility at their disposal, and not in a construction zone.
Golden City blends modern architecture with a curated, sustainable, and green living space, allowing residents to live in harmony with nature. Surrounded by the romantic Long Xuyen River, the development offers a unique living experience in the heart of An Giang, whilst preserving the ‘proud and liberal’ nature of the Southwest.

Legal

  • Investment Certificate of An Giang People's Committee No. 521031000391 issued on May 19, 2015
  • Decision of approving the 1/500 plan of 1405 / QĐ-UBND of An Giang People's Committee issued on 09 May 2017.

Land price: fluctuating from 18-40 million/m2 depending on the location.
House price: 3.5-storey building and finishing surface: 4.5 million / m2

Payment procedure: Deposit: 100 million VND / 01 floor
Stage 1: At least 10 days from the date of deposit and upon signing the purchase contract: 30% of the total contract value (VAT included and deposit)
Stage 2: Within 30 days from the payment of Stage 1: 15% of the total value of the contract (VAT included)
Stage 3: Within 60 days from the payment of Stage 1: 15% of the total contract value (VAT included)
Stage 4: Within 90 days from the payment of Stage 1: 15% of the total value of the contract (VAT included)
Stage 5: Right after signing transfer contract. 20% of the total contract value When completing the procedures for submission of land use right transfer dossiers at competent state agencies in Long Xuyen. 5% of the total contract value (VAT included)
Note: If the payment 4 & 5 overlap, the investor will notify customers 15 days before.
