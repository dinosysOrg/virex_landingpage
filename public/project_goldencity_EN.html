Golden City An Giang Urban Area
My Hoa Ward, Long Xuyen City, An Giang Province
PROJECT TYPE
A complex of offices, sports, services, trade fairs and residential areas
INVESTOR
Trading and Service Joint Stock Company (of Alphanam Group)
PRICE
Land price: 18 – 40 million/m2
House price: storey-builing: 3.5 million/m2 and finishing surface: 4.5 million / m2
INTRODUCTION
Living the good life should be attainable for everyone. Golden City achieves this through blending modern architecture with a curated, sustainable, and green living space, allowing you to live in harmony with nature. Surrounded by the romantic Long Xuyen River, Golden City offers a high-class living experience in the heart of An Giang, whilst preserving the ‘proud and liberal’ nature of the South-West.

Dong A Trading and Service Joint Stock Company is a member of Alphanam Group, one of the leading multi-sector economic groups in Vietnam. With the desire to build a symbolic urban area in Long Xuyen City, Dong A strives to offer residents of the Golden City An Giang project a unique location of the highest quality. With more than 20 years of experience in the market, the strong growth and financial gains of investors are evidence of the quality of the project and the progress made at the An Giang Golden City project.

Construction progress

To invest in the construction of Golden City urban area under the cultural, sports, service, exhibition and residential complex with the area of 43.39 hectares, including 02 stages as follows:

  • Phase 1: 22.5 ha
  • Phase 2: 20.89 ha

The project will be implemented from 2015 to 2016. In which:

  • Phase 1:
    • Quarter II / 2015 to Quarter III / 2015: Preparation for investment
    • From Quarter III / 2015 to Quarter II / 2016: Implementation of investment projects
    • Quarter III / 2016: Putting the project into operation
  • Phase 2:
    • Quarter II / 2015 to Quarter III / 2015: Preparation for investment
    • From Quarter III / 2015 to Quarter II / 2016: Implementation of investment projects
    • Quarter III / 2016: Putting the project into operation.

Note: If the payment 4 & 5 overlap, the investor will notify customers 15 days before.
