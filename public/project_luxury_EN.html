INTRODUCTION
Luxury Apartment offers guests large living spaces and broad views towards My Khe beach and the mountains of Da Nang. Located on Vo Nguyen Giap, adjacent to a plot of land to be developed as a park, Luxury Apartment offers guests a private spot on the beach that is quiet, away from the road, yet central enough to explore the city. It is the perfect way to experience life in Da Nang, known for its high quality of life.

With a bustling tourism industry and fast growing economy, Da Nang is seeing a growth that is unmatched by other cities in Vietnam, consistently reaching double digits. With the current growth rate of Da Nang, it will develop not just as a tourist destination but as a global business destination as well.

Alphanam Luxury Apartment is owned by Alphanam Real Estate Joint Stock Company; one of the top 10 most reputable Real Estate firms in Vietnam.

Alphanam Group is developing a series of projects across Vietnam with their partners Marriott International and the InterContinental Hotels Group. Together they bring Vietnam a wealth of experience and are raising the bar for Vietnam’s Hospitality Industry.

  • Designer: Luxury Apartment is designed by Salvador Pérez Arroyo and partners. With architectural work done by 79Pro Vietnam and DPA Singapore.
  • Main Architect: Salvador Perez Arroyo
    • Nationality: Spanish
    • Top 10 famous architects in the world
    • Over 40 years of experience
    • Awarded with more than 50 prestigious awards
    • Published many books on architecture
    • Postmodern architecture style
  • Apartments’ Public Areas Managed by: Savills Group (With over 20 years of real estate management experience in Vietnam and managing over 65 million square meters of housing and commercial space worldwide).

Construction contractor: Delta Civil and Industrial Construction Company

DELTA was established in 1993, specializing in contracting construction of civil and industrial projects. DELTA works with high quality, technical and aesthetic requirements such as hotels, office complexes and private houses.

Over the past 25 years of construction and development, DELTA has become a large construction corporation with 12 member companies, combining 2500 engineers and architects with state-of-the-art equipment.

Situated on the road that proudly bears the name of General Vo Nguyen Giap,with Pham Van Dong beach and Bien Dong park nearby, Luxury Apartment possesses one of the most desirable locations in Da Nang. With its prime location on My Khe Beach, Luxury Apartment’s ability to connect guests with the tourism spots of Da Nang is second to none. Son Tra Peninsula, Linh Ung Pagoda, Song Han Bridge, Hoi An Ancient Town and Hue are close by and are few of the must see places when visiting Da Nang city. Also, the international airport is 10 minutes and Da Nang CBD is just 5 minutes away.

Distances:

  • City International Airport: 5Km
  • Center of Danang city: 3km
  • Han River Dragon Bridge: 3 Km
  • Son Tra Peninsula: 3 Km
  • Linh Ung Pagoda: 4 Km
  • Ba Na Hills Tourist Area: 20km
  • Hoi An Ancient Town: 22Km
  • Da Nang Train Station: 4 Km

Alphanam Luxury Apartment has 9 different apartment types designed in a 1 or 2 bedroom layout, with all rooms boasting beautiful views towards either Pham Van Dong beach, downtown or the majestic Son Tra Peninsula.

  • 1 bedroom apartment: 58m2 - 65m2
  • 2 bedrooms: 73m2 - 75m2 - 79m2 - 102m2

Alphanam Luxury Apartment is fully furnished with modern high-quality amenities and managed by a professional international management team in order to provide a comfortable living experience.

Facilities include:

  • Infinity pool

  • Restaurant
  • Rooftop bar/li>
  • 24/7 Security
  • Reception

  • High-speed Wi-Fi

Luxury Apartment enjoys a unique location right on the beach of My Khe - one of the five most charming beaches on the planet. Considered to be the most desirable resort paradise in Vietnam, annually attracts large number of visitors and become an ideal destination for events of the coastal city of Da Nang.

The Alphanam Luxury Apartment project is interlinked to the Bien Dong Park and the prolonged Vo Nguyen Giap route, which makes it easy to travel, connect to the international airport areas and the administrative center of Da Nang.

Paying for Luxury Apartment Danang is divided into 2 payment periods:

  • Stage 1: 5 days from the date of deposit: 50% of the selling price (including VAT and deposit)
  • Stage 2: 7 days from the first payment date. Payment of the remaining amount and 2% for the apartment maintenance fee.
