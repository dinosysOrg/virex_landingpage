VIREX aims to strengthen the relationship between:

VIREX – CUSTOMERS - DEVELOPERS – PARTNERS

Vietnam’s real estate market is still too unprofessional. With an abundance of inconsistent images and false information, friction between sellers and buyers is inevitable. This friction leads to an unsafe feeling when purchasing real estate in Vietnam and does not benefit a healthy environment for domestic as well as international sales.

VIREX will offer a reliable platform with one clear gateway; from developers to VIREX to you. All real estate listings on the platform are easy to find, categorized and VIREX Verified. This way you know that what you are buying, is exactly what you are getting.

  • Mission

    Mission

    To provide a transparent, secure, and easy to use real estate platform, on which developers and sellers of real estate can showcase their listings and reach potential buyers and investors.

  • Vision

    Vision

    To become the prime facilitator of real estate sales in Vietnam within 5 years’ time.

  • Core Value

    Core Value

    People and technology

    By investing in modern technology, VIREX will help sales people reach their sales targets faster and more reliably. This allows VIREX to reach higher sales volumes compared to traditional sales methods.

    The VIREX Partnership Model ensures benefits for all; it allows VIREX to combine forces with the most professional sales people, the right advisers, and to move away from the ‘private game’ that is played now. Transparency, Clarity and Security, this is what is required to be VIREX Verified.

Projects
Eden Rose Thanh Trì
In the heart of Hanoi, The Eden Rose unfolds as its villas and terraced houses spread out as eight species of roses with eight fragrances and eighteen different colors. Residents can walk leisurely on the streets, filled with flowers and greenery, and immerse themselves in this unique haven in Hanoi.
Golden City An Giang
The Golden City project in An Giang is currently in the process of selling land plots in stages. These plots are to be developed by Alphanam Group, or the land plot owner can assign their own developer should they wish to do so. The project is divided up into several sectors, each starting construction once all plots have been sold in their respective sector.
Laluna Nha Trang
The condotel La Luna Resort is built on a prime location in Nha Trang, with a total area of 4.74ha located right in front of the most expensive beach area of Nha Trang City. When La Luna will officially be put into operation, the project will add 1977 5-star apartments of international standard to the market in order to meet the fast-growing need for high-quality resorts in Vietnam.
Luxury Da Nang
Luxury Apartment offers guests large living spaces and broad views towards My Khe beach and the mountains of Da Nang. Located on Vo Nguyen Giap, adjacent to a plot of land to be developed as a park, Luxury Apartment offers guests a private spot on the beach that is quiet, away from the road, yet central enough to explore the city. It is the perfect way to experience life in Da Nang, known for its high quality of life.
