INTRODUCTION
Taking roses as inspiration of the main building idea for the villas and garden, Vimefulland chose for her third child the name Eden Rose - red velvet roses with classic beauty, timeless, the most beautiful but also the most familiar species In the heart of Hanoi, The Eden Rose exists as a lovely painting of eight species of roses with eight fragrances and 18 different colors. Coming to this place, residents can walk leisurely on the streets filled with passionate flowers, relaxed and immersed in the romantic space in the capital.

  • Eden Rose’s investor is the largest pharmaceutical corporation in Vietnam: Vimedimex. Established in 1984, in the early days of Ho Chi Minh City, Vimedimex provides products and services of the best quality for the customers and communities they serve.

    • Established: Vimedimex Pharmaceutical Joint Stock Company (Vimedimex Pharma) in 1984. (33 years)
    • Total staff: 1000 staff
    • Subsidiaries: pharmaceuticals (06), real estate (22), finance (02)
    • 2016 revenue: 14 trillion VND
    • Projects implemented: The Eden Rose Thanh Liet, Citylight Building in Ho Chi Minh City, The Emerald Center My Dinh Park, Vimedimex Building, Belleville Hanoi

    With the business philosophy "Build full of Trust, work hard from the Heart", Investors of The Eden Rose Thanh Tri built the brand Vimefulland into a successful sustainable real estate company providing the best health care to their customers.

  • The Eden Rose villa design unit is a design company from France; NKB Archi, with a strength in modern neo-classical architecture
  • The decision for NKB Archi has been carefully considered by investors, based on previous projects and a consensus regarding the appreciation for development of green environmentally-friendly real estate products.
  • Vimedimex created a project that can withstand the times; with its neoclassical architecture and spacious design, as well as health care facilities.
  • Managed by: Savills Group
    Savills Group (With 20 years of real estate management experience in Vietnam and managing over 65 million square meters of housing and commercial space worldwide).
  • The Eden Rose’s main contractor is Ecoba Vietnam Joint Stock Company, with their strength: constructing the most sustainable real estate products of today.
  • Ecoba has given the project a completely different level of construction, as all technology used by this company for the construction of the Eden Rose project is the most advanced in the market.
  • Past projects of Ecoba in Hanoi include Ecopark, Park Hill and The Emerald.
  • Prime location just 10km from Hanoi center. Situated on a land area of 8 hectares in Thanh Liet Commune - Thanh Tri District - Hanoi City.
  • The Eden Rose is strategically important for the expansion and development of the capital. According to the plan for future development of Hanoi, Thanh Tri will become one of the main district from 2020 to 2025.
  • The location of The Eden Rose Thanh Tri is close to many of Hanoi's main infrastructure, such as: Ring Road 3, Linh Dam Street and Kim Giang. Here you can easily connect with other districts in the city and neighboring provinces; such as Bac Ninh, Vinh Phuc and Hung Yen.
  • The Eden Rose Thanh Tri connects to the modern urban chain in the West and South-East of Hanoi such as the high-class Park City, Van Phu urban area and Duong Noi. Just adjacent to the project facilities such as K Hospital, Ha Dong Station, and Military Medical Hospital are available.
  • ‘The Eden Rose’ got its name from French architect Francois Barberot. The project consists of 52 semi-detached villas and 181 terraced houses built and designed in a luxurious neo-classical style, with a hint of modernity.
  • Housing sizes range from 82-218m2 for the 181 terraced houses and 130-300m2 for the 52 semi-detached and lone-standing villas.
  • he shophouses are located directly on the main road to make it easier to reach customers and residents. Stairs are arranged along the length of the shophouses to help reduce the required space, as well as to increase ventilation for the homes so the owner can operate their business more easily/offer the space for rent.
  • The project aims to offer residents the ideal life of "Living Luxuriously and Harmoniously within Nature". The project offers owners 1ha of green space of the total construction area of ​​8ha.
  • The Eden Rose project has many facilities such as a medical station, hospital, and a sports complex with swimming pools, tennis courts, badminton and jogging area.
  • Similar with most other 5-star projects, The Eden Rose offers up to 30% of the land used to construct utility buildings such as a kindergarten, BBQ garden, commercial service area and a smart-parking lot.
  • The Eden Rose’s security system is a multi-layered security system with fencing around the campus, reinforced with CCTV. Most notably, there are well-trained and highly qualified security guards. This is a highly rated standard at The Eden Rose, and a USP.

Construction progress

  • Currently, 11/2017 projects The Eden Rose have all the construction permits and officially launched to the market at a specific price.

Deposit: 100 million VND / unit

Stage 2: 20% of the contract value is due on the expected date of building the 2nd floor of the real estate product (VAT included)
Stage 3: Payment of 20% of contract value on the expected date of roofing the house (QIII / 2018, VAT included)
Stage 4: Payment of 25% of contract value + Maintenance fee on the date of handing over the house (Including VAT)
Stage 5: Payment of 5% of the contract value within 5 days with notice of handover of the Certificate of land use right and ownership of assets attached to land (VAT included)
