PROGRAM FOR FOREIGNERS AND OVERSEAS VIETNAMESE

PURCHASING HOUSES IN VIETNAM

CONDITIONS FOR BUYING HOUSES IN VIETNAME OF FOREIGNERS AND OVERSEA VIETNAMESES
  • For overseas Vietnamese:

    Whoever are allowed to enter Vietnam (with legal entry visa).

  • For foreign individuals:

    Whoever are allowed to enter Vietnam and are not eligible for diplomatic or consular privileges or immunities according to the provisions of law (visa entry + commitment not subject to diplomatic or consular immunity).

  • For FDI Enterprises, branches and representative offices of foreign enterprises, foreign investment funds and foreign bank branches:

    Being operating in Vietnam (with investment certificate, branch establishment permit, lawful office);

TRANSACTION PROCEDURE:
  • 1.Customers choose apartments/villas that they want and place the deposit directly at the Developer's Sales Office or the Agent to register to buy.
  • 2.The developer signs a deposit agreement with customers, customers pay the deposit at the time of signing the deposit agreement; within 10 days, customers have to sign a purchase contract with the developer.
  • 3.The customer pays the purchase price of the apartment / villa under the purchase contract to the developer's account opened in Vietnam. The payment currency is VND.
  • 4.Upon completion of the construction, the developer shall send a notice of delivery of the apartment/villa according to schedule to the customer in accordance with the terms of the purchase contract; The customer accepts the transfer of the apartment/villa in accordance with the terms of the purchase contract (after paying the full amount stated in the purchase contract)

3.6. Can foreigners use credit cards to pay the deposit?

Foreigners can use foreign credit cards (but note that they must be international credit cards) to pay for purchases and sales of goods and services in Vietnam. And the payment transaction must be made in VND, in case the price is recorded in freely convertible foreign currency, the agreement must be converted into VND in the contract.

Note

This issue was adjusted in the Consolidated Document No. 04 / VBHN-NHNN dated April 22, 2014 incorporating Decision No. 20/2007 / QD-NHNN and Circular No. 23/2011 / TT-NHNN.
