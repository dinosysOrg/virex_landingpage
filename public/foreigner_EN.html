PURCHASING HOUSES IN VIETNAM

CONDITIONS FOR BUYING HOUSES IN VIETNAME OF FOREIGNERS AND OVERSEA VIETNAMESES
  • For overseas Vietnamese:

    Whoever are allowed to enter Vietnam (with legal entry visa).

  • For foreign individuals:

    Whoever are allowed to enter Vietnam and are not eligible for diplomatic or consular privileges or immunities according to the provisions of Vietnam law.

  • For FDI Enterprises, branches and representative offices of foreign enterprises, foreign investment funds and foreign bank branches:

    Being operating in Vietnam with an investment certificate, branch establishment permit and a registered office

Note:
These conditions are in line with the Law on Housing, issued and effective from 1 July 2015, as detailed in No. 99/2015 / ND-CP dated October 20th, 2015.

TRANSACTION PROCEDURE:
  • 1. The Customer chooses a real estate product and places a goodwill deposit with VIREX to register intent of purchase. (Deposit = 100 million VND).
  • 2. VIREX transfers the goodwill deposit and registers the Customer to the Seller.
  • 3. The Seller will sign a deposit agreement with the Customer for the official deposit (the official deposit is detailed in the Seller’s sales policy).
  • 4. The Customer pays the deposit stated in the sales policy.
  • 5. The Seller sends an invitation to the Customer to sign the purchase contract.
  • 6. The Customer pays the purchase price of the real estate product as detailed in the purchase contract, to be paid directly to the Seller’s account in Vietnam. The payment currency is VND.
  • 7. Upon construction completion, the Seller sends the Customer a notice of delivery of the real estate product in accordance with the terms of the purchase contract. The Customer accepts the transfer of the real estate product in accordance with the terms of the purchase contract (after paying 95% contract price stated in the purchase contract).
  • 8.8. The Seller prepares the red book (Land Used Right Certificate) for the real estate product in the Customer’s name (See section 3.4).

Note:
The Customer’s identification documents must include: (1) Consular legalization in Vietnam; (2) Visa for Vietnam must be valid at the time of signing the contract. Visa exemption applies for Foreigners with Vietnamese spouses, children are Vietnamese or overseas Vietnamese). Regulations on Visa exemption as per Decision 135/2007 / QD-TTG

THE PROCEDURE OF SELLING HOUSES FOR FOREIGNERS AND OVERSEAS VIETNAMESE

1. Conditions for buying houses in Vietnam of foreigners and overseas Vietnamese

  • For overseas Vietnamese: Whoever are allowed to enter Vietnam (with legal entry visa).
  • For FDI Enterprises, branches and representative offices of foreign enterprises, foreign investment funds and foreign bank branches: Being operating in Vietnam with an investment certificate, branch establishment permit and a registered office.
  • For foreign individuals: Whoever are allowed to enter Vietnam and are not eligible for diplomatic or consular privileges or immunities according to the provisions of Vietnam law.

Note

These conditions are in line with the Law on Housing issued and effective from 1 July 2015, as detailed in No. 99/2015 / ND-CP dated October 20, 2015.

2. Transaction Procedure

  • The Customer chooses a real estate product and places a goodwill deposit with VIREX to register intent of purchase. (Deposit = 100 million VND).
  • VIREX transfers the goodwill deposit and registers the Customer to the Seller.
  • The Seller will sign a deposit agreement with the Customer for the official deposit (the official deposit is detailed in the Seller’s sales policy).
  • The Customer pays the deposit stated in the sales policy.
  • The Seller sends an invitation to the Customer to sign the purchase contract.
  • The Customer pays the purchase price of the real estate product as detailed in the purchase contract, to be paid directly to the Seller’s account in Vietnam. The payment currency is VND.
  • Upon construction completion, the Seller sends the Customer a notice of delivery of the real estate product in accordance with the terms of the purchase contract. The Customer accepts the transfer of the real estate product in accordance with the terms of the purchase contract (after paying 95% contract price stated in the purchase contract).
  • The Seller prepares the red book (Land Used Right Certificate) for the real estate product in the Customer’s name (See section 3.4).

Note

The Customer’s identification documents must include: (1) Consular legalization in Vietnam; (2) Visa for Vietnam must be valid at the time of signing the contract. Visa exemption applies for Foreigners with Vietnamese spouses, children are Vietnamese or overseas Vietnamese). Regulations on Visa exemption as per Decision 135/2007 / QD-TTG

3. Legal issues related to the process of selling housing to foreigners and related procedures / matters

The Customer can choose one of the following three options:

  1. The Customer opens an account with a Foreign Bank Branch in Vietnam which has a presence in the Foreigner’s country of choice, and pays the sum to the account of the Seller through said account. Visit https://goo.gl/QPEm6W for a list of Foreign Bank Branches in Vietnam. (See note 1)
  2. The Customer transfers money directly from abroad into the Seller’s account in Vietnam.
  3. The Customer deposits cash (in VND) into the Seller’s account at the bank account stated in the purchase contract.

Note 1

  • Option (1) is preferred should the Customer intend to invest in Vietnam real estate more frequently. When income is placed in this account, it will suffice as proof of funding for future bank loans. See 3.11. for more information on bank loans.
  • Foreigners can only carry a certain amount of foreign currency into Vietnam. When bringing foreign currencies into Vietnam, customs procedures must be adhered to.
  • When purchasing real estate, the amount of foreign currency transferred into Vietnam cannot exceed the value of the transaction (the price listed on the real estate contract).
  • If a foreigner transfers money and accepts to pay all the fees, the recipient will pay the fee of "have money into the account" and the fee of “foreign exchange" if they want to withdraw money in foreign currency. If a foreigner pays only a portion of the fee or does not pay the fee, the recipient will pay the outstanding fee for the transfer as charged at the foreign bank.
  • Transactions related to foreigners' accounts opened at banks are closely controlled by the Bank through the checking of documents, the purpose of conducting transactions, checking the identity of the account holder, and the signature of the account holder on the money transfer / debit authorization.

Note 2

Regulations on opening bank accounts in Vietnam: Circular 23/2014 / TT-NHNN regulates the operation of accounts in Circular 16/2014 / TT-NHNN and this account is allowed to conduct transactions. Use cash in accordance with Decree 101/2012 / ND-CP, Circular 46/2014 / TT-NHNN

The Customer can select the following options:

  1. Set up a VND account at a commercial bank in Vietnam (or an existing account opened at a Vietnam bank) to make the payment for the real estate product. This account will receive the revenue made from the investment.
  2. Have real estate developers and leasing companies transfer revenues directly to overseas accounts.

The Seller, or leasing companies, will deduct from the turnover taxes and fees following the provisions of the purchase contract and the law of Vietnam:

  • Contractor's tax (personal income tax) on turnover: for property leasing services is 5% of income
  • Value added tax for foreign individuals: 5% of turnover for VAT calculation
  • Remittance fee + electricity charge + foreign bank charge (if any)

Note 1

The amount of foreign currency transferred out of the territory of Vietnam will not exceed the lawful income of the individual at the price stated in the lease contract (converted from VND) or the price written on the contract of transfer of real estate between foreign individuals and the transferee.

Note 2

Contractor's tax stipulated in Article 5, Article 12 of Circular 103/2014 / TT-BTC. Corporate income tax for foreign organizations is stipulated in Circular 103/2014 / TT-BTC.

The tax rate applicable to property leasing business entities stipulated in Article 10 of the Personal Income Tax Law of 2007 is amended and supplemented by Law No. 71/2014 / QH13 (Article 2.4) Regulations on banking charges are regulated in Circular 26/2013 / TT-NHNN

  • VAT upon purchase The apartment is 10% of the selling price of the apartment (excluding the value of land use rights and the cost of maintaining the area under common ownership 2% of the selling price of the apartment). VAT upon purchase Villa is 10% of the villa price (excluding the value of land use rights).
  • For apartment: The cost of maintaining the area under common ownership accounts for 2% of the price of the apartment.
  • Registration fee for applying for a certificate: 0.5% of the value of the property for registration fee, however, the maximum fee is 500 million VND per property / registration. The value of assets for registration fee calculation is the price set by the provincial People's Committee from time to time.
  • State administrative fees and charges when applying for a certificate: According to the State's regulations from time to time, now is VND 661,000.
  • Personal income tax: 2% of transfer price.
  • VAT of the foreign contractor under item 3.2.
  • Administrative fees and charges (if the seller accepts to pay on behalf of the buyer): according to the State's regulations from time to time, it is VND 661,000.
  • Registration fee for applying for a certificate: 0.5% of the value of the property for registration fee, however, the maximum fee is 500 million VND per property / registration. The value of assets for registration fee calculation is the price set by the provincial People's Committee from time to time.

Foreigners can only carry a certain amount of foreign currency into Vietnam. When bringing foreign currencies into Vietnam, customs procedures must be carried out.

Note

Under regulations, if the amount of cash brought by foreigners to Vietnam is more than $ 5000, then the customs declaration.

Foreigners can use foreign credit cards (but note that they must be international credit cards) to pay for purchases and sales of goods and services in Vietnam. The payment transaction must be made in VND. In case the price is recorded in freely convertible foreign currency, the agreement must be converted into VND in the contract.

Note

This issue was adjusted in the Consolidated Document No. 04 / VBHN-NHNN dated April 22, 2014 incorporating Decision No. 20/2007 / QD-NHNN and Circular No. 23/2011 / TT-NHNN.

The Seller will request the Customer to submit the dossier as a red book (Land Use Right Certificate) in accordance with the provisions of the PL when the parties have completed the handover and settlement of the land

The developer shall carry out red book procedures for clients within the time limit prescribed by law, from the date the clients submit the complete dossiers under the notice. The red book is issued by the State (about 3-6 months since the full application).

Duration of house ownership:

  • For Overseas Vietnamese: Same laws apply as local Vietnamese, ownership is for life.
  • For foreigners: 50 years of ownership and a one-time extension of the house ownership term at the request of the owner, but cannot exceed 50 years from the first expiration of the house ownership (Item 2 of Article 161 of the Housing Law and Article 77 of Decree 99).
  • For foreign organizations: Adheres to the duration of the investment certificate in Vietnam.

The sample used in the sale of housing basically consists of the following documents:

  • Deposit Agreement and Subscription (before the foundation), or Deposit Agreement (in case of post-foundation deposit).
  • Sales contract and enclosed appendices. The contract of sale and purchase according to the registered form (regardless of foreigners and Vietnamese), translated into English, form the contract. In case of a dispute, the Vietnamese version will be followed.

According to Article 144 of the Housing Act 2014, effective from 01.07.2015, owners of real estate in Vietnam are organizations entitled to mortgage their houses with credit institutions operating in Vietnam. Owners of dwelling houses are individuals who may mortgage their houses at credit institutions or economic organizations operating in Vietnam or individuals as prescribed by law. As such, the law of Vietnam does not currently provide for cases where foreigners are entitled to mortgage their houses overseas as collateral, but only the right to mortgage their properties as housing for the Bank.

Overseas Vietnamese can authorize others to sign the sale contract and related documents. Authorization documents must be made at the private notary office in foreign countries and be legalized by the Vietnamese consular and translated into Vietnamese in Vietnam or authorized by the Ambassador of Vietnam in foreign countries with the passport / ID card of authorized person.

Condition:

  • Must prove income to pay principal + loan interest
  • Must have a labor contract in Vietnam
  • Must have a guarantee from a foreign bank for a loan in Vietnam
  • Must have income from collateral (rental, turnover, etc)

Loan rates are determined on a case-by-case basis.

  • Ensure construction is in accordance with the approved planning and construction standards of Vietnam (recorded in the contract).
  • 5-year structure warranties for apartments and 2-year for villas.
  • Equipment is accompanied by the manufacturer's standard warranty.
