INTRODUCTION
The condotel La Luna Resort is built on a prime location in Nha Trang, with a total area of ​​4.74ha located right in front of the most expensive beach area of Nha Trang City. When La Luna will officially be put into operation, the project will add 1977 5-star apartments of international standard to the market in order to meet the fast-growing need for high-quality resorts in Vietnam.
The Swisstouches La Luna Nha Trang Apartments are designed in a modern European architecture style and offers a high-class living space. La Luna aims to be one of the best holiday destinations to enjoy as a family in Vietnam.

  • The project is invested by Marina Hotel JSC with its head office located at Nguyen Co Thach Street, Vinh Hoa Urban Area, Vinh Hoa Ward, Nha Trang City, Vietnam.
    Ben Du Thuyen Hotel Joint Stock Company is a relatively new name, but has attracted the attention of numerous real estate investors in Nha Trang with condotel Swisstouches La Luna Resort 5-star international standard. By choosing carefully the prime location with prestigious units for investment and strategic partnership, the investor of Ben Du Thuy Hotel Joint Stock Company believe that they will use their strengths in Real Estate to fulfill the trust and bring the effectiveness to the investors.
  • Navigate to the style of exquisite design, unique and world-class, Ben Du Thuyen Hotel Joint Stock Company initially succeeded in cooperate with the world's leading hotel management brand Swisstouches Hotels & Resorts Group for managing the Swisstouches La Luna Resort luxury resort in Nha Trang to provide customers with excellent experiences and perfect service quality.
  • Managed by: Swisstouches Hotel & Resort:
    Swisstouches Hotel & Resort is a luxury hotel management brand from Switzerland, by using the system of 18,000 hotel and resort named Swisstouches reach 5 star standards with research centers of cuisine, events, hotels which linked with 500,000 thousand travel agents to create a network of transnational booking, the occupancy rate is always reach 70%

Designers: Grandland Group

  • Leading Design Company in Hong Kong. The company has 20 years of experience with more than 1,500 design professionals in Hong Kong and abroad
  • The company won 500 design awards in Hong Kong and the world.
  • Typical Design Projects: Guangzhou Grandview Marriott Hotel, Huizhou Egret Lake Sheraton Agile Sheraton Resort Hotel, Shan Hai Tian Marriott Hotel Sanya, Huzhou Sheraton Hot Sping Resort Hotel...

Executing unit: Tan Ky Construction and Real Estate Corporation

  • Tan Ky Construction and Real Estate Corporation (TAKCO) is a well-known brand name in the field of construction and installation in Vietnam with many key projects in major cities nationwide.
  • TAKCO has achieved ISO 9001-2000 quality management system and many prestigious certificates and prizes in the country.
  • Typical projects have been implemented: Sam Sung Electronics Factory, Da Nang Administrative Center, Azura Danang luxury apartment complex, Dai Quang Minh II villa area, Kingston Residence...
  • The diamond position is located at the foot of Tien mountain, the position of "Mountain Based, Ocean View" along the gold line Tran Phu - Pham Van Dong
  • Located near the scenic spots: Ponagar Tower, Hon Chong...
  • In front is the first international marina in Vietnam
  • Project La Luna Resort is a perfect design even in a small detail which helps clients can feel the luxurious, elegance, sumptuous but equally delicate and full of vibrant in the way that only few projects that currently have. Condotel La Luna is divided into 2 blocks
  • Block A will be located 2 towers of 35-storey with 5 floors of commercial and service with low construction density accounting for only 62% of total land area.
  • Block B has a 35-storey apartment building and a basement with a construction density of only 59%. Apartment blocks in the project Condotel Nha Trang are arranged from 1 to 4 bedroom apartments go along with luxury penthouse will certainly meet the needs of customers.

With 100% Seaview apartment, luxurious architectural style, sophistication and unique, Project La Luna Resort bring to the owner no obstacles in front with the most comfortable environment possible. When you open the curtains, or stand out on the balcony, visitors can also embrace the sea, enjoy the cool breeze wind from the blue ocean.

At the first4 floors are innovative in design and utility services, each floor is a different distribution of utilities such as:

  • Gym, Spa
  • Shopping area, commercial center
  • Cinemas, amusement parks, parks
  • Meetings or events
  • Luxury, professional restaurant system

The next 30 floors is a floating tower style that creates a space immersed in the sea at dawn or gentle afternoon sunset. Each apartment with interior design system is professionally suited to the space of the room and needs of the owner

Construction progress

At present, the project has completed the foundation and is in the construction stage, all workers and machinery are actively working day and night to complete the project as scheduled. The project will start operations in Q1/2018 as planned.
